About EJIVE

Ejive is a National level Technical symposium,Initiated by pragati engineering college Organized by Electronics and Communication Department as a part of under annual technical event "Strides".Ejive commenced with an objective of uncovering the impeccable and hidden talents of young calibers to exhibit indigineous inventions through this technical extravaganza.Ejive makes the best splendid experience and vivacious moments with the perfect blend of Knowledge.
